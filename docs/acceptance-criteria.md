Acceptance Criteria
 Project: Bank Chatbot Support System

 User Story: US-001 – Ask Banking Questions

Acceptance Criteria (AC-001)

Scenario: Customer asks a banking question.

Given the customer has opened the bank chatbot,

When the customer asks a question about banking services,

Then the chatbot should provide a correct and relevant answer.

User Story: US-002 – Check Account Balance

 Acceptance Criteria (AC-002)

Scenario: Customer checks account balance.

Given the customer is successfully logged in,

When the customer requests their account balance,

Then the chatbot should display the correct account balance.

User Story: US-003 – Report Lost ATM Card

Acceptance Criteria (AC-003)

Scenario: Customer reports a lost ATM card.

Given the customer has a registered bank account,

When the customer reports a lost ATM card through the chatbot,

Then the chatbot should confirm the report and initiate the card-blocking process.

User Story: US-004 – Transfer Customer to Human Agent

 Acceptance Criteria (AC-004)

Scenario: Chatbot cannot answer a customer's question.

Given the chatbot cannot provide a suitable answer,

When the customer requests further assistance,

Then the chatbot should transfer the conversation to a bank support agent.

User Story: US-005 – Update Chatbot Knowledge Base

Acceptance Criteria (AC-005)

Scenario: Administrator updates chatbot information.

Given the administrator is logged into the system,

When the administrator updates the chatbot's knowledge base,

Then the chatbot should use the updated information when responding to future customer questions.
